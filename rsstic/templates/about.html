About

rsstic ("rustic", "rss ticker") is a service for aggregating and moderating rss feeds into a single, cleaned up rss output. Create a new project and add feeds to it, selecting only those items within the feeds that you want in the project's aggregated RSS output. Re-generate your project's RSS after moderation.

RSStic is built using Django, mostly as a fun learning exercise for me about that framework. I also think the ability to curate a set of feeds by adding and subtracting individual items is useful.
